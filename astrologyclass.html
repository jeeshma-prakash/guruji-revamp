Key Concepts in Astrology

  • Zodiac Signs

    The 12 divisions of the sky that the Sun moves through in a year (Aries, Taurus, Gemini, etc.)

  • Natal (Birth) Chart

    A map of the sky when you were born; used to understand your traits and life path.

  • Planets

    Each planet represents different energies (e.g., Venus = love, Mars = action).

  • Horoscope

    A chart or prediction based on your birth time and date

  • Houses

    12 parts of the sky that represent areas of life (career, family, love, etc.)

  • Aspects

    Angles between planets; they show how planets interact and affect you.

Types of Astrology

Western Astrology

Based on the tropical zodiac (mostly used in Europe/America)

Vedic Astrology (Jyotisha)

Ancient Indian system based on the sidereal zodiac

Chinese Astrology

Based on birth years and lunar cycles with animal signs

Mayan or Egyptian Astrology

Other ancient systems with different foundations
